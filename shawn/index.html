IS SHAWN OKAY?

The Star

The Star: Hope, guidance, clarity. You are okay. There is light and direction in your path, even if it's not always visible.

WHAT SHOULD SHAWN TRUST?

Ten of Disks

Ten of Disks: Long-term legacy, family, generational wealth. In the long run, you're going to have a family and a legacy. Trust that this will come through, no matter how much you worry about it. It's already written in your future.

WHAT SHOULD SHAWN SUSPECT?

Eight of Disks (Reversed)

Eight of Disks Reversed: Stalled progress, uneven effort. Suspect that progress might not be moving as actively or as proactively as you'd hope. There's a lack of full-force momentum. The stability you need feels stalled sometimes—not moving at full capacity or with the urgency you feel is necessary.

SHEA'S INNER WORLD

Shea's Intentions - Four of Disks

Four of Disks: Stability, security, grounded foundation. Her intentions are grounded and stable. She have four legs—whatever she's building is secure and balanced. This is solid ground.

Shea's Feelings - Wheel of Fortune (Reversed)

Wheel of Fortune Reversed: Bad luck, stalled cycles, unachievable goals. It's like she won the lottery but can't cash it out. The wheel has stopped turning. She's stuck in a cycle where victory feels within reach but unreachable.

OUTCOME WITHOUT CHANGES

Nine of Wands

Nine of Wands: Defensive, paranoid, almost finished but stalled. This shows an infinite stalemate. You're almost there, but you're not there. You're paranoid and frazzled. No progress—just an endless, stalled loop. This is the path if nothing changes.

CHANGES NEEDED FOR DIFFERENT OUTCOME

Change Shea Should Make - The Hierophant

The Hierophant: Structure, tradition, spiritual foundation. She needs to lean more towards established structure and spiritual grounding. She should find stability through tradition, ritual, or foundational beliefs. This is the shift needed.

Change You Should Make - Seven of Wands

Seven of Wands: Standing your ground, having the upper hand, victory against opposition. Maybe it's realizing that you have the upper hand and not everybody's against you. You can stand firm. You have strength in this position.

OUTCOME WITH CHANGES

The Devil

The Devil: Bondage, attachment, material focus, shadow self. If you make these changes, the outcome shows The Devil—a wake-up call about attachments and patterns you've been bound to. It's a catalyst for breaking free from what holds you back.
